Marine Refrigeration Services

Temperature Control and Climate Control

In controlled climate environments, refrigeration is responsible for maintaining the appropriate temperature for perishable or sensitive cargo, such as food, chemical products, and liquefied gases.

Depending on the ship's configuration, the same refrigeration plant or independent units can be used to serve different supply areas and refrigerated compartments.

Purpose of the Refrigeration System

The main objective of the marine refrigeration system is to ensure that perishable cargo and materials are transported in safe conditions, preserving their quality and integrity throughout the voyage.

  • Prevention of microorganism growth
  • Reduction of oxidation processes
  • Control of unwanted fermentation
  • Prevents dehydration and cargo deterioration

CRANE SERVICES

Winch Maintenance on Ships

Most winches on board are driven by electric motors or by pressurized hydraulic oil, allowing the lifting of various loads with the help of gears.

Regular inspection of gears and shafts allows for the evaluation of machinery wear and use. Our specialized team performs detailed inspections on winch and rudder hydraulic systems, checking for:

  • Pressure loss
  • Leaks
  • System safety

Performing an overload test ensures that the service is safe and efficient.
