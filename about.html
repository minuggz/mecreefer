About Mec Reefer

Mec Reefer has a solid trajectory in the maritime sector, built over more than 25 years of experience focusing on quality, trust, and responsibility. Founded in Santos, São Paulo — where its headquarters remain today — the company has expanded its port and maritime services to serve vessels in different regions of Brazil and worldwide.

National and international operations, with onboard teams offering fast responses and efficient solutions.

Long-standing service provider for the Ministry of Defense, providing continuous technical support for vessels and equipment.

Complete technical and operational support, including vessel modernization, life extension, and data management.

In the commercial division, Mec Reefer has a repair workshop strategically located just a few meters from the port, equipped with modern, high-performance machinery, ensuring agility, precision, and efficiency in maintenance and repair services.

Mec Reefer's growth is guided by its commitment to clients and collaborators, maintaining high standards of health and safety, respect for the environment, and excellence in workmanship.
